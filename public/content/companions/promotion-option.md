##  Recommended Rolls

Typically the player will benefit from having companions with full pages of max roll for attack. If the player is lacking mana recovery (ie. running out of mana when using fulg) it would help to add one or two rolls of mana recovery.

### Options

* Extra ATK(%) : 3 / 4 / 6 / 9 / 14 / 20  
* CRIT Dmg(%) : 5 / 7 / 10 / 15 / 24 / 35  
* Extra HP(%) : 5 / 7 / 10 /15 / 24 / 35  
* Extra HP Recovery(%) :  5 / 7 / 10 /15 / 24 / 35  
* Extra Mana(%) : 3 / 4 / 5 / 8 / 10 / 15  
* Extra Mana Recovery(%) : 3 / 4 / 5 / 8 / 10 / 15  
* Monster Gold(%) : 3 / 4 / 6 / 9 / 14 / 20  
* Accuracy : 3 / 4 / 5 / 8 / 10 / 15  
* Dodge : 3 / 4 / 5 / 8 / 10 / 15  
* Extra Exp(%) : 1 / 2 / 3 / 4 / 5 / 8  
* CC Resist : 3 / 4 / 6 / 9 / 14 /20



### Probabilities

Gray is 23% / Green is 20% / Orange is 30% / Purple is 20% / Red is 5% / Blue is 2%

### Additional Information  

At [Ether] Promotion the player unlocks the ability to roll for the highest option available. This allows the player to change all promotion options for the selected companion to the maximum possible for 40,000 dice. Once an option has been selectedm it cannot be undone.
